LegacyLink — A Modern "Grief Tech" Platform

Freelance — Full-Stack Architect & Solo Developer

legacylinkstudio.com ↗

A full-stack "Grief Tech" platform that bridges physical and digital memorialization. Weatherproof ceramic tombstone plaques become interactive living memorials — visitors scan a high-durability QR code to instantly access a mobile-first biography, photo gallery, interactive timeline, and digital guestbook.

  • Dynamic Edge Routing Layer: Architected an ultra-low-latency short-URL redirect system using Fastify and Redis caching at the edge.   • Real-Time Memorial Builder: Designed a multi-step admin wizard (portrait upload → life-timeline builder → media & privacy settings) with a synchronized live-preview pane that updates in real time — letting families see exactly how the final memorial will render before publishing.
  • Analytics, Ordering & Premium Features: Shipped a comprehensive operations dashboard tracking physical scan-counts, geofenced scan locations, and plaque fulfillment pipelines. Premium modules include an interactive family tree panel, GPS-navigable burial mapping for large cemeteries, and a rich-media digital guestbook with custom lazy-loading image pipelines for instant mobile speeds on cellular cemetery networks.
